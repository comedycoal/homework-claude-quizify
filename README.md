# Quizify

Test your knowledge with rapid-fire trivia questions. Answer fast, beat the clock, and compete for the top spot on the leaderboard.

## Features

**Challenge Yourself**
- 20+ quiz categories spanning science, history, geography, pop culture, and more
- Difficulty levels ensure questions get progressively harder
- Answer 4 multiple-choice options per question

**Race Against the Clock**
- 15 seconds per question—time pressure keeps you sharp
- Live countdown timer shows how much time you have left
- Auto-advance if you run out of time

**Earn Points & Build Streaks**
- 1 point per correct answer
- Get 3 correct in a row? Your next points are **doubled**
- Hit 5 in a row? Your next points are **tripled**
- Streaks reset on a wrong answer, so stay focused

**See Your Performance**
- Running score visible throughout the quiz
- Final results show your accuracy percentage and total time
- View your score, ranked against your previous attempts

**Compete Globally (Locally)**
- Leaderboard displays the top 10 all-time scores
- Your best scores are automatically saved
- Come back anytime to beat your high score

## Quick Start

```bash
# Open in your browser:
open index.html

# Or run a local server:
python3 -m http.server 8000
# Then visit http://localhost:8000
```

## How to Play

1. Click **"Start Quiz"**
2. Read the question carefully
3. Click an answer before the timer runs out (15 seconds)
4. Watch your score climb—go for the streak bonus!
5. After the last question, see how you ranked
